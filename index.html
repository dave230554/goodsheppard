Meet Our Doctors!

Dr. Tasha Thavarajah

Dr. Tasha Thavarajah became a Chiropractor to pursue her passion for helping and educating others on maintaining optimal health and wellness. Dr. Thavarajah earned her Bachelors in Health Science and later earned her Doctorate in Chiropractic. Dr. Thavarajah helps people of all ages in providing the best care of Chiropractic and nutritional counseling in order to maintain overall health and wellness. She has several years of experience in treating patients with musculoskeletal conditions and working with MVA (Motor Vehicle Accident), WSIB (Workplace Safety and Insurance Board) and EHC (Extended Health Care) patients. In her practice, she provides therapeutic treatments such as ultrasound therapy, electrotherapy, soft tissue therapy/manual therapy, taping, cupping, laser therapy and spinal manipulative therapy. Each treatment will be tailored to suit the specific need of the patient.
